Power law model for one-dimensional mass distribution with a Gaussian component.

\[ \begin{align}\begin{aligned}p(m) &= (1 - \lambda_m) p_{\text{pow}} + \lambda_m p_{\text{norm}}\\p_{\text{pow}}(m) &\propto m^{-\alpha} : m_\min \leq m < m_\max\\p_{\text{norm}}(m) &\propto \exp\left(-\frac{(m - \mu_{m})^2}{2\sigma^2_m}\right)\end{aligned}\end{align} \]
