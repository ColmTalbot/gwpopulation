gwpopulation.utils.beta_dist¶

gwpopulation.utils.beta_dist(xx, alpha, beta, scale=1)[source]¶

Beta distribution probability

\[p(x) = \frac{x^{\alpha - 1} (x_\max - x)^{\beta - 1}}{B(\alpha, \beta) x_\max^{\alpha + \beta + 1}}\]
Parameters
xx: float, array-like

The abscissa values (\(x\))

alpha: float

The Beta alpha parameter (\(\alpha\))

beta: float

The Beta beta parameter (\(\beta\))

scale: float, array-like

A scale factor for the distribution of the distribution (\(x_\max\))

Returns
prob: float, array-like

The distribution evaluated at xx
