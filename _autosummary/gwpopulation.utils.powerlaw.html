gwpopulation.utils.powerlaw¶

gwpopulation.utils.powerlaw(xx, alpha, high, low)¶

Power-law probability

\[p(x) = \frac{1 + \alpha}{x_\max^{1 + \alpha} - x_\min^{1 + \alpha}} x^\alpha\]
Parameters
xx: float, array-like

The abscissa values (\(x\))

alpha: float, array-like

The spectral index of the distribution (\(\alpha\))

high: float, array-like

The maximum of the distribution (\(x_\min\))

low: float, array-like

The minimum of the distribution (\(x_\max\))

Returns
prob: float, array-like

The distribution evaluated at xx
