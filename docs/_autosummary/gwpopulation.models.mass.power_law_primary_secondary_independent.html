gwpopulation.models.mass.power_law_primary_secondary_independent

gwpopulation.models.mass.power_law_primary_secondary_independent(dataset, alpha, beta, mmin, mmax)[source]

Power law model for two-dimensional mass distribution, modelling the primary and secondary masses as following independent distributions.

\[ \begin{align}\begin{aligned}p(m1, m2) &= p_{\text{pow}}(m1) p_{\text{pow}}(m2) : m1 \geq m2\\p_{\text{pow}}(m) &\propto m^{-\alpha} : m_\min \leq m < m_\max\end{aligned}\end{align} \]
Parameters:
dataset: dict

Dictionary of numpy arrays for ‘mass_1’ (\(m_1\)) and ‘mass_2’ (\(m_2\)).

alpha: float

Negative power law exponent for more massive black hole (\(\alpha\)).

beta: float

Negative power law exponent of the secondary mass distribution (\(\beta\)).

mmin: float

Minimum black hole mass (\(m_\min\)).

mmax: float

Maximum black hole mass (\(m_\max\)).
