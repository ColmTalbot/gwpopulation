gwpopulation.models.mass.three_component_singleÔÉÅ

gwpopulation.models.mass.three_component_single(mass, alpha, mmin, mmax, lam, lam_1, mpp_1, sigpp_1, mpp_2, sigpp_2, gaussian_mass_maximum=100)[source]ÔÉÅ

Power law model for one-dimensional mass distribution with two Gaussian components.

\[ \begin{align}\begin{aligned}p(m) &= (1 - \lambda_m) p_{\text{pow}}(m) + \lambda_m p_{\text{norm}}(m)\\p_{\text{pow}}(m) &\propto m^{-\alpha} : m_\min \leq m < m_\max\\p_{\text{norm}}(m) &\propto \exp\left(-\frac{(m - \mu_{m})^2}{2\sigma^2_m}\right)\end{aligned}\end{align} \]
Parameters:
mass: array-like

Array of mass values.

alpha: float

Negative power law exponent for the black hole distribution.

mmin: float

Minimum black hole mass.

mmax: float

Maximum black hole mass.

lam: float

Fraction of black holes in the Gaussian components.

lam_1: float

Fraction of black holes in the lower mass Gaussian component.

mpp_1: float

Mean of the lower mass Gaussian component.

mpp_2: float

Mean of the upper mass Gaussian component.

sigpp_1: float

Standard deviation of the lower mass Gaussian component.

sigpp_2: float

Standard deviation of the upper mass Gaussian component.

gaussian_mass_maximum: float, optional

Upper truncation limit of the Gaussian component. (default: 100) Note that this applies the same value to both.
